Chicken Pesto Pasta

Description:

This chicken pesto pasta is the perfect dish to make your tongue palletes dance. It comes with a delightful fusion of tender chicken pieces, rich and creamy pesto sauce, made from fresh basil and garlic. Want to add a slight kick? Then sprinkle the beautiful, glorious dish with red pepper flakes! Perfect for any occassion, this pasta is sure to leave you craving more.


Ingredients:
  • 1 (16 ounce) package of pasta of your choice
  • 1 teaspoon olive oil
  • 2 minced garlic cloves
  • 2 skinless, boneless chickem breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • (optional) 1 or more pinch of crushed red pepper flakes
  • 1/2 cup of pesto sauce
  • 1/3 cup of oil packed sun-dried tomatoes, drained and cut into strips

Steps:
  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook until al dente, 8-10 minutes; drain.
  3. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Saut√© garlic until tender.
  4. Stir in chicken and (optional) season with red pepper flakes. Cook until chicken is golden and cooked through.
  5. Combine pasta, chicken, pesto, and sun-dried tomatoes in a large bowl; toss to coat evenly.
